Fettucine Noodle

Description

Fettucine noodles are my favorite noodle to make on Earth. It is mouthwatering goodness, and I cannot seem to ever resist the urge when it arises! Here is the recipe I follow, I hope you enjoy it as much as I do. This recipe can be scaled up/down, just add/subtract 100 grams of 00 flour per 1 egg. The recipe laid out for you today is good for about 2-3 people.

In order to prepare Fettucine, you must first source the best ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 00 Flour
  • Farm Fresh Eggs
  • A bit of water
  • Pinch of salt

and last but not least, ELBOW GREASE!

Step by step!

  1. Start out by setting 2 eggs out till they hit room temp. This is very important.
  2. Once that is done, make a mound out of 200 grams of flour, then form a volcano.
  3. Beat both eggs in, whilst slowing bringing in flour from the sides.
  4. Add a pinch of salt, and continue to whisk in flour. Once you have achieved a sort of dough, start to knead it together.
  5. Knead for about 10-15 minutes and continue to add water when necessary. When done, cover dough with plastic wrap and place inside refridgerator for 30 minutes.

That's it! Now all that is left to do, is roll out the dough, as thin as you can, then cut the noodles or use a pasta making machine. Hang to dry. Freeze or put in boiling pot of heavily salted water for 90-120 seconds. Fresh pasta cooks quick!
