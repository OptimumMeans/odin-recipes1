Fettucine Alfredo

In order to prepare Fettucine, you must first source the best ingredients.

  1. 00 Flour
  2. Farm Fresh Eggs
  3. A bit of water
  4. Pinch of salt

and last but not least, ELBOW GREASE!

Start out by 2 setting eggs out till they hit room temp. This is very important. Once that is done, make a mound out of 200 grams of flour, then form a volcano. Beat both eggs in, whilst slowing bringing in flour from the sides. Add a pinch of salt, and continue to whisk in flour. Once you have achieved a sort of dough, start to knead it together. Knead for about 10-15 minutes and continue to add water when necessary. When done, cover dough with plastic wrap and place inside refridgerator for 30 minutes.
