Pesto Cavatappi

Description

This is a dish I have been wanting to try for some time now, and in making this webpage for it, I just might have to try it!

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. cavatappi pasta noodle
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 20 oz. package boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1 inch cubes
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 large Roma tomatoes, seeded and chopped (about 1 cup)
  • 1 7 oz. container refridgerated pesto sauce
  • 3/4 cup heavy whipping cream

Steps

  1. Cook and drain pasta as directed on box
  2. Meanwhile, grab a 12-inch skillet and heat olive oil over medium-high heat
  3. Add chicken, salt, and pepper; cook 5 to 7 minutes.
  4. Stir occasionally, until browned and no longer pink, stir in tomatoes.
  5. Reduce heat to medium; cook 3 to 4 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Stir in pesto sauce and cream; cook 2 to 3 minutes longer.
  7. Stir in pasta, tossing to coat the noodles.
